About

Claire O’Shaughnessy is a soprano from Columbus, OH. This spring she will graduate from the Bienen School of Music where she studies with Pamela Hinchman.

At Northwestern, she has performed as Valencienne cover (The Merry Widow), The Dew Fairy (Hansel & Gretel), Sylvianne (The Merry Widow) and First Spirit (Die Zauberflöte) with Northwestern Opera Theater and as La fée (Cendrillon) and Eurydice (Orpheus in the Underworld) with Northwestern OPUS. This spring she will perform Woman 1 in the first staged production of Liza Sobel Crane’s I DID, DID I? with Northwestern’s Cosmia Opera Collective.

She also spent two summers at La Musica Lirica festival in Novafeltria, Italy, where she studied Italian language, diction and opera. She performed the roles of Susanna cover (Le Nozze di Figaro), Riccardo (Il trionfo dell’onore) and Anina (La Traviata).

Claire is also a journalism student at Northwestern University’s Medill School of Journalism. She will graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Journalism, as well as a Bachelor of Music in Voice & Opera.
